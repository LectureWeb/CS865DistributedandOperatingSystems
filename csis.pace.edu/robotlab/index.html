Welcome to Pace University's Robotics Lab. Supervised by Dr. Benjamin, the Robotics Lab research project focuses on building and testing the ADAPT robot cognitive architecture based on the Soar cognitive architecture. Check out some video clips.

We are also building intelligent agents for network security and intrusion detection. Please feel free to browse around to find out more about our work.

We are grateful to Dr. Tom McKenna and the Office of Naval Research for selecting us for the 2019 Defense University Research Instrumentation Program (DURIP). This grant provides advanced robots to support our work on human-robot collaboration, including the robot shown above.
